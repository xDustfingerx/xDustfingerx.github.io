EMF level 5 ElectroMagnetic Field Reader - MUST hit a level 5 reading to confirm. Will only peak at 5 when Ghost is interacting in environment.
Spirit Box Must have the lights off and be near the ghost for it to answer. If the ghost is shy, it will require you to be the only person in the room.
Fingerprints A ghost will leave behind a finger print when it interacts with a door, interacts with a light switch, or knocks on a window. You can see fingerprints by using the UV Light or the Glowstick. *Note* Footprints seen with UV do not count s Fingerprints
Ghost Orb Visible in the computer screen using a Video Camera or a Head Mounted Camera with night vision. Ghost Orbs start emitting at the start of the round, and will continuously emit throughout the round and looks like a very small yellow floating orb.
Spirit Writing If Ghost Writing is a piece of evidence, the ghost has a chance to interact with the Ghost Writing Book. You can increase the Ghost Activity in the room to increase this chance.
Freezing Temps A room can appear to be as low as 3 Celsius, but if you see it drop any further then you can guarantee that Freezing Temperatures is a piece of evidence. *Note* Almost all ghost make rooms cold nut few make it below freezing.
